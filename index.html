Our story

Built on the Takumi principle: the hand of the master.

“Takumi” (匠) describes the craftsman who has mastered every detail of their trade. We carry that principle onto the production floor — disciplined processes, continuous improvement (kaizen), and a team empowered to stop the line when quality is at risk.

  • IATF 16949 & ISO 14001 aligned quality and environmental systems
  • Integrated APQP, PPAP, and FMEA from part concept to launch
  • Dedicated program managers for every OEM and Tier 1 customer
