Our story

Built on the Takumi principle: the hand of the master.

“Takumi” (匠) describes the artisan who has mastered every detail of their trade. As the Canadian subsidiary of Takagi Manufacturing Company, Ltd. of Okazaki, Japan, we carry that principle onto the production floor — disciplined processes, continuous improvement (kaizen), and a team empowered to stop the line when quality is at risk.

  • DMAIC & Six Sigma principles applied to every program
  • Integrated APQP, PPAP, and FMEA from part concept to launch
  • Dedicated program managers for every OEM and Tier 1 customer

What we stand for

Five commitments that shape every shift.

The five pillars we hold ourselves to — shared across every Takagi Group plant — define how we treat our people, our customers, and the communities we operate in.

Safety

A genuinely safe workplace is our first daily metric. Operators are empowered to stop the line whenever safety is at risk.

Quality
